About

Resume

Software engineer looking for an on-site position with plenty of opportunities for growth. Currently living in NYC but willing to relocate.

Prior to coding, I taught math during the school year and taught Spanish and French at a language immersion camp over the summer. I've always loved learning and intellectual challenges, and while teaching was a good fit for that, coding is even better!

I'd done some coding independently before, but this fall I enrolled in App Academy so I could focus 100% on coding and really develop my skills. I've spent over 1000 hours pair programming programming, working on projects (below), and learning about topics such as test-driven development, object-oriented programming, abstract data structures, scalability, RESTful APIs, and many others.
